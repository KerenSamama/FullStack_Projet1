Outdoor Activity

Fall Night Illumination

Niagara Falls is impressive by day but it’s vibrant and colorful at night when it’s lit up. Enjoy a tour of the illumination of American Falls, Bridal Veil Falls, and Horseshoe Falls after the sun goes down. Your guide will tell you all about the falls’ interesting history as they take you to the best lookout points, including Luna Island. Read more about Niagara Falls Night Illumination Tour: American, Bridal and Horseshoe Falls

Helicopter Flight

Experience Niagara Falls like never before, from an entirely new perspective, on this scenic helicopter journey. Enjoy the incredible sight of the American, Bridal Veil, and Canadian Horseshoe Falls from high above, along with a spectacular view of the many landmarks along the way, like the Niagara River and Whirlpool Rapids. Each passenger receives an individual headset that provides interesting and educational commentary about the falls during the flight. The view is unforgettable, but bring a camera to capture the moment. Read more about Niagara Falls CANADA Helicopter Tour

See Sight Tour

Some Niagara Falls tours are American and some are Canadian. Not this one. Get the full experience from both sides of the border on this deluxe tour of everything Niagara Falls has to offer. Visit all the must-see Niagara Falls attractions, from the thrilling Maid of the Mist boat cruise to the exciting Cave of the Winds walk—even a trip to the top of the Skylon Tower and much more. All attraction and admission fees, plus hotel transfers and road tolls are included. Food and beverages are at your own expense. Read more about Niagara Falls in One Day: Deluxe Sightseeing Tour of American and Canadian Sides

Maid of the Mist

Get as close as possible to Niagara Falls on this escape to one of the world’s most incredible natural wonders. Feel the powerful rush of the falls from the Prospect Point Observation Tower, overlooking the American falls. Take a journey to the edge of the rushing water — by boat on the Maid of the Mist and by land on the Cave of the Winds walking adventure. Read more about Niagara Falls American-Side Tour with Maid of the Mist Boat Ride

Seva Tapas and Bar is located at The Cadence Hotel at 200 Rainbow Blvd, Niagara Falls, NY, 14303. Seva debuts its sultry and innovative bar offering artisanal cocktails, paired with seasonally focused small plates menu. An al fresco dining atmosphere with luxe outdoor lounge seating, which sets the tone for an extraordinary experience in the heart of Downtown Niagara Falls, NY.

The Western Door Steackhouse

From the moment you taste that first succulent bite of grilled Delmonico, grass-fed ribeye or cast-iron porterhouse, the Western Door will instantly become your new favorite Niagara Falls steakhouse. The award-winning Western Door Steakhouse offers guests a premier dining experience, with an irresistible menu of choice steaks wet-aged a minimum of 28 days. Rounding out the menu are dishes featuring the freshest pork, chicken and seafood available. In to mouthwatering appetizers, salads, sides and entrées, enjoy the finest selection of wines from the nearby Finger Lakes region of New York State and around the world. Located inside the Seneca Niagara Resort & Casino.

310 4th St, Niagara Falls, NY 14303

+1 716-299-1100
